Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the roots

The usuallu deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combinated with any salad vegetable.
